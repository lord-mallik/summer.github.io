School Programs

Summer - Winter School Program offers interactive short courses twice a year in various domains. The courses are designed to provide an exhaustive and in-depth knowledge. All courses explore theory and practical perspectives which will help students to gain more from, and contribute more through working life. The program covers city sightseeing. We also arrange weekend trips on prior request.

Cross Cultural Management Avenues

  • Understand basic concept of corporate culture.
  • Identify the impact of global business environment on corporate Culture & business.
  • Apply theoretical frameworks in analyzing culture & related Management problems.
  • Recognize how cross cultural communication differences affect business decision Making.
  • Understand negotiation and leadership skills required across cultures and borders.

CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate ) Routing and Switching

  • Build simple LANS, perform basic configurations for routers and switches, and implementing IP addressing schemes.
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills using real equipment and Cisco Packet Tracker.
  • Configure and troubleshoot routers and switches and resolve common VLAN routing issues in both IPv4 and IPv6 networks.
  • Configure WAN technologies and network services required by converged applications in a complex network.

Industrial Internship

RK University is situated in Rajkot city which is the economic, industrial and educational hub of the region. Engineering and Auto ancillary industry is viewed as the growth engine of the district. Textiles and Apparels is also an emerging sector. Ajanta, world’s largest clock manufacturer is present in the Rajkot district. Rajkot produces various fruits, vegetables and spice crops of 31577,296813 and 200262 M.T., respectively. Rajkot is having micro, small, medium and large enterprise having an investment of around USD 2240 Million. Industrial zone of Rajkot is having variety of clusters like machine tool, engineering, ceramic industry, bearing, foundry, automobile parts, pumps set, gems & jewellery, agriculture equipment manufacturing, plastic manufacturing, textile and handicraft clusters which exports their items across the world in almost all domains. RK University provides an industrial internship in all sectors within Rajkot and outside of the Rajkot also.
